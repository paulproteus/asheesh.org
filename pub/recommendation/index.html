The Process

Individuals were invited by Shauna to contribute to the letter via a wiki she set up for the project. After creating the wiki and inviting contributors, Shauna stepped back. She asked Asheesh Laroia and Marina Zhurakhinskaya to edit the contributions into a cohesive and compelling letter.

Asheesh and Marina used the following editing process:

  • They sorted all the comments into categories such as "Shauna the communicator" or "Shauna and technology".
  • They created an outline, with several categories corresponding to each item in the outline.
  • They wrote the letter, splitting up editing duties by paragraph.
  • Asheesh used HTML tags to produce a colorized version, and tweaked the colors and opacity to make them as unobtrusive as possible.
  • They asked the contributors to check that they were represented accurately and provide the editors with their affiliations.

To read the full text of any individual's contribution, you can view the letter's page history.
